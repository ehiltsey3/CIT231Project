     Herbert Randall Wright III, better known by his stage name G Herbo, is an American rapper from Chicago. G Herbo was born on October 8, 1995 being 27 years old as of today. G Herbo has three kids known by Emmy, Essex, and the oldest sitting at four years old, Yosohn.

     As one of Chicago's most renowned drill artists, G Herbo—formerly known as Lil Herb—relates his experiences in a rough-hewn, urgent, and frequently painful manner. He stands for the South Shore district of his city, more particularly for the neighborhood known as Terror Town. He made his musical debut in 2014 with Welcome to Fazoland, Humble Beast in 2017, and Swervo in 2018. He continued to climb the Billboard 200. PTSD from 2020, 25 from 2021, and Survivor's Remorse from 2022, all of which debuted in the Top Ten, were G Herbo's subsequent albums and continued his run of mainstream success.
